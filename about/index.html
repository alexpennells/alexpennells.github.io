i'll be doing this as long as i'm alive.

Creating games to me is just as fun as playing them. I always have some sort of game-related side project and I have no plans to stop that trend. During university I decided to take a bit of a break from creating larger projects but now my goal is to release a game on Steam before the end of 2016. I'm not one to play favorites though, I hope to release my games on as many platforms as possible.

i still got a whole lotta love for music.

It's rare to find me working in silence as I like to have a soundtrack to my life. I'm a big fan of variety so I never limit myself to a single genre. From Tupac to Bob Dylan - Daft Punk to Iron Maiden, I don't discriminate. The songs that I listen to inspire both my design and my development, so I never like to work without some background tunes.

just me.
